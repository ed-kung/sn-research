After running regression \eqref{eq_zreg}, we inspected the embedding dimensions that were most predictive of zaps. The first most predictive dimension appears to capture meta-posts (not necessarily in the \texttt{meta} territory) about Stacker News itself, such as tips for how to post and earn more sats.
